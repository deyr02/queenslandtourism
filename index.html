Places in Queenstown

Surrounded by the soaring indigo heights of the Remarkables and framed by the meandering coves of Lake Wakatipu, Queenstown is a right show-off. Looking like a small town, but displaying the energy of a small city, it wears its ‘Global Adventure Capital’ badge proudly, and most visitors take the time to do crazy things that they’ve never done before. No-one’s ever visited and said, ‘I’m bored’.

Upcoming Events in Queenstown

Bridie Henderson: Porcelain Black and White

Date:17 September - 12 October 2016

Since bursting onto the New Zealand art scene in 2011, Bridie Henderson's work has mainly concerned the subject of feathers. Henderson’s feathers are created from delicately worked porcelain and displayed in handmade wooden cases that resemble traditional museum displays. They are softly backlit to show off their finely worked detail and their delicate, fragile nature.

Hammers and Horsehair

Date:23 September 2016, 7:30pm

Two well-known New Zealand musicians perform a programme of Classical and early Romantic pieces on period instruments, transporting the audience to another place and time.

#ROXYFITNESS

Date:25 September 2016, 10am - 2pm

The #ROXYfitness tour invites women of all backgrounds and fitness levels from across the globe to join an epic day of fitness fun, to run, stand-up paddle board and practice yoga, in celebration of leading an active outdoor lifestyle.
